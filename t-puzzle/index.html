四巧板 • T-Puzzle Challenge

四巧板，又称“T字之谜”。仅用四块不规则木板，即可拼组成上百种图案，而最具挑战性的正是“T”字形。

在游戏中锻炼空间想象力、观察分析力和逻辑判断力。挑战一下？

The T-Puzzle is a classic four-piece tangram. Use just four irregular pieces to form hundreds of shapes—the ultimate challenge is the letter "T".

Sharpen your spatial imagination, observation skills, and logical thinking. Ready for the challenge?
