I came to the US in 1988 from China and have stayed in Reno, Nevada the whole time since I love this small and beautiful place. I received both of my master and PhD degrees in computer science from University of Nevada, Reno. During my time in Reno, I worked for small firms, large corporations, did computer consulting, and startup. Now I am teaching computer science in the Computer Science and Engineering department of University of Nevada, Reno. Teaching is one of the best thing one can do because you work with young people. Teaching is also one of the most challenging thing to do since you are mentoring young people and they are all different with their own talent, strength, weakness, different live experience and different culture background.
